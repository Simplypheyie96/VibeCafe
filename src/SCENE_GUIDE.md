## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Audio Not Playing
- **Cause**: Browser autoplay policy
- **Solution**: Click anywhere on the page first, then try controls
- **Alternative**: Manually adjust any volume slider to trigger playback

### Scene Transitions Feel Laggy
- **Cause**: Too many active effects
- **Solution**: Disable some interaction icons before switching
- **Note**: Cross-fade takes ~1 second for smooth transition

### Ambient Sounds Cut Off
- **Cause**: Audio file loading timeout
- **Solution**: Use the retry button in the error notification
- **Tip**: Try adjusting volume slider to re-trigger loading
