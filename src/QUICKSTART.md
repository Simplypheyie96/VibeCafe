## Troubleshooting

### Music not playing?
- Click the play button in the controls modal
- Check your browser allows autoplay (some browsers block it)
- Try clicking the orb first, then play

### Ambient sounds not working?
- Make sure sliders are above 0
- Check browser audio permissions
- Try refreshing the page

### Carousel feels choppy?
- Reduce "Motion" intensity in mood settings
- Close other browser tabs
- Update your browser

### Icons not appearing?
- Hover over the wallpaper slowly
- Some scenes have icons in different positions
- Look for subtle glowing elements

## Advanced Features

- Enjoy pure wallpaper + music experience
- The orb stays available for quick access
